---

## 📚 Purpose
This repository is my personal notebook of cybersecurity learning and growth.  
It’s meant to capture:
- CTF insights & writeups
- College course notes
- Certification study material
- Practical lessons learned from real-world experiences

---

## 🗂️ Structure
Each folder organizes a different area of focus:
- **CTF-Notes** → Tactics, payloads, and writeups from competitions (NCL, TryHackMe, HackTheBox, etc.)  
- **College-Courses** → Summaries, exam prep, and coursework notes.  
- **Certifications** → Study prep for Security+, CySA+, SSCP, Pentest+, etc.  
- **Life-Lessons** → Reflections, workflows, and professional development notes.
